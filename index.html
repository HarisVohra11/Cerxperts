Our Services

Detailing

Our auto detailing services keep your vehicle in the best possible condition by removing both visible and invisible contaminants from the vehicle’s interior while polishing the exterior to that “fresh out of the showroom” look.

Price: $150

Auto Collision Repair

Our trained and experienced trained automotive technicians fix the damaged parts and bodies of the vehicles which have been caused by accidents in car collisions, incidents, or weather conditions. Our services in our auto collision repair services involve dent removal, scratch removal, glass replacement, paint matching and many more.

Price: $200 for inspection + service costs

Paint

Our trained and experienced technicians in our paint service department can help you restore your car to showroom condition using only the highest quality paint such as SPEEDOKOTE and DUPLI-COLOR manufactured paints.

Price: $2,000 for full body paint.

Oil Changes

Our veteran automative technicians will remove and replace the old oil running in your internal combustion engine and replace it with fresh new motor oil.

Price: $300

Michael Scott

Jimothy Halpert

Stanley Hudson
